Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a traditional Italian dish consisting of layers of flattened pasta interleaved with sauce, cheese (such as Bocconcini or Parmesan), and various meats.

Ingridients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 2 large onions, finely chopped
  • 5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 cups marinara sauce
  • 4 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 8 lasagna noodles (fresh or pre-cooked)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions until al dente.
  3. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef with chopped onions and minced garlic over medium heat.
  4. Remove the meat mixture from the skillet and add marinara sauce to the same pan. Simmer for 15 minutes.
  5. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  6. Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish with butter or cooking spray.
  7. Layer the cooked lasagna noodles, meat sauce, mozzarella cheese, and vegetables in the prepared baking dish.
  8. Cover with aluminum foil and bake for about 30 minutes.
  9. Remove the foil and bake an additional 25-30 minutes until the top is golden brown.
