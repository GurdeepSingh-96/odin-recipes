Zuppa Inglese

Description

Zuppa Inglese, also known as English Soup, is a classic Italian dessert consisting of soaked stale bread layered with poached whole eggs and a mascarpone cheese sauce.

Ingridients

  • 4 cups stale bread slices
  • 1 cup milk
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 1/2 cup mascarpone cheese at room temperature
  • 2/3 cups granulated sugar, divided
  • Vanilla extract (optional)

Steps

  1. Soak the bread in milk until soft and slightly mushy.
  2. In a saucepan, whisk egg yolks and sugar over medium heat until it thickens and takes on a custard-like texture.
  3. Add mascarpone cheese to the yolks milk mixture and stir gently but thoroughly.
  4. Pour the prepared sauce onto the soaked bread layer in a baking dish.
  5. Top with a whole poached eggs (slice them in half or as needed).
  6. Place the baking dish in the oven at 350°F for about 15 to 20 minutes.
  7. Serve warm, optionally drizzled with vanilla extract.
