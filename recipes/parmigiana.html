Parmigiana

Description

Parmigiana is a classic Italian dish where sliced breaded eggplant is layered with mozzarella cheese, then fried in oil to form a crispy omelette sandwich.

Ingridients

  • 4 large eggplants
  • 2 cups crushed fresh tomatoes or tomato paste
  • 3 medium onions, finely chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup Italian bread crumbs (pan di Spagna)
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil

Steps

  1. Cut the eggplants lengthwise into thin slices and place them on a baking sheet.
  2. Drizzle with olive oil and salt. Roast in preheated oven (375°F or 190°C) for about 20-30 minutes until they are golden brown and soft.
  3. Sauté chopped onions in a skillet over medium heat until translucent.
  4. Add crushed tomatoes and garlic to the pan, simmering for 10-15 minutes until the sauce thickens.
  5. Combine bread crumbs, Parmesan cheese, eggs, and salt to make a coating mixture.
  6. Dip each eggplant slice into the mixture, ensuring it's evenly coated all over.
  7. Heat sufficient oil in a deep skillet over medium heat. Fry the eggplant slices in batches until golden and crispy.
  8. Serve hot with marinara sauce on the side.
